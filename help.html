Help - Report Voting Fraud

Need Help or Want to Report Voting Fraud?

We take voting fraud seriously. If you believe there has been any irregularities, please report it immediately. We aim to ensure a safe and transparent voting process.

Here's what you can do:

  • Report any suspicious voting behavior or fraud.
  • Ask for assistance if you're having trouble casting your vote.
  • Get answers to frequently asked questions (FAQs) about the voting process.

Common Voting Irregularities

  • Voter impersonation: Someone voting on behalf of another person.
  • Double voting: A person voting more than once.
  • Fraudulent voter registration: Fake or fraudulent individuals being registered to vote.
  • Vote buying: Offering money or incentives in exchange for votes.
  • Ballot box stuffing: Illegally adding extra ballots to the ballot box.
  • Misleading information: Providing voters with false or misleading information to influence their vote.
  • Unlawful assistance: Providing unauthorized assistance to voters during the voting process.
